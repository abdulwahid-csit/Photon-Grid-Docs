Welcome to the **Photon Grid** blog. Photon Grid is a fast, feature-rich JavaScript data grid with sorting, filtering, grouping, editing, selection, pagination, integrated charts, sparklines, and theming.

This blog is where we share release notes, tips, and deep dives into building high-performance data grids.
